How to Play:

1. Choose a pit on your side to collect and distribute stones

2. If your last stone lands in your store, you get another turn

3. If your last stone lands in an empty pit on your side, you capture that stone and all stones in the opposite pit

4. Game ends when all pits on one side are empty

5. The player with the most stones wins
